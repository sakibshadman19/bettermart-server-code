BetterMart is a powerful e-commerce website with advanced features for secure shopping. I have implemented password hashing, JWT authentication, and authorization to protect your account. Forgot your password? No worries! I've created a reset system and confirmation emails for your convenience.

Discover personalized product recommendations with my smart recommendation system based on machine learning. Manage your shopping easily with Redux for smooth interactions and Stripe for secure payments. My advanced search and filters help you find what you need. Leave ratings and reviews to help others.

For admins, I offer an intuitive dashboard to manage products, orders, and users. You can update passwords and view purchase history with your own dashboard. I've also created a comprehensive client book that provides project details.
